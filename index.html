Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Professor Yanxun Xu. Before I joined Johns Hopkins, I received my bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Sichuan University. I am broadly interested in developing Bayesian methods for various statistical problems and applications, including electronic health records, dynamic treatment regimens, and precision medicine in HIV.
